<h3>not your typical B-school kid.</h3>

I'm at IIM Ranchi doing BBA — but most of what I care about happens outside the classroom. I enter competitions and usually place. I build web projects nobody asked me to build. I got into AI workflows early and actually use them. I like figuring out why things work and then making them work better.

I think the gap between *having a good idea* and *actually shipping it* is where most people stay permanently. I'm trying not to be one of them.

**Right now:** building, competing, learning fast.

<br/>

**What I'm good at:**
- turning a vague problem into a clear plan
- making data tell a story people actually want to hear
- brand and marketing strategy that doesn't feel like a template
- moving from idea → execution without losing the thread
